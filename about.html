Viksha Tech Solutions

Established in August 2024 and headquartered in Chirala, Andhra Pradesh, Viksha Tech Solutions (OPC) Private Limited is a registered one‑person company committed to contributing to the education sector. Under the leadership of Managing Director Uma Maheswararao Macharla, the company operates with a focus on integrity, innovation, and growth. Though in its early stages, Viksha Tech Solutions is legally and financially structured to scale, with its registered capital and compliance in place.
